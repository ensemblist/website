In and out of sleep all day, with nothing to eat. 

Blocked nose, sore throat, and swollen glands, 

Difficulty swallowing, numb hands and feet.  

Burning and sweating then freezing and fretting.  

Spaced out, unable to concentrate - fog and migraines. 

In nerve, muscle, and bone. Sharp stabbing pains.  

What did I do to deserve this suffering?  

A phone call with the insurer on how things are going.
